● How the Dashboard Works

  The data — what's real vs. mocked

  Real (from your database):
  - Physicians Enrolled — actual count of rows in the CampaignEnrollment table
  for that campaign
  - Messages Sent — enrolled × sequence steps. If you have 3 physicians and a
  2-step email sequence, that's 6 messages
  - Enrollment table — every physician's name, specialty, affiliation, and
  status (contacted / replied / bounced) pulled live from the DB
  - Status badge (Draft / Active / Completed) — real campaign status

  Mocked (hardcoded, not real):
  - Open Rate (41.3%) — fixed placeholder. A real system would track email open
  pixels
  - Replies — floor(enrolled × 0.18) — 18% of enrolled, fake
  - Meetings Booked — floor(enrolled × 0.08) — 8% of enrolled, fake
  - Bar chart — completely fixed: [3, 8, 12, 9, 15, 6, 4] for Mon–Sun. It
  doesn't change based on anything. A real system would log a timestamp every
  time an email is sent and group by day

  The status flow

  When you launch a campaign, physicians go into the DB as contacted. In a real
  app, when someone replies to your email, a webhook would update their status
  to replied. bounced would come from email delivery failures. Right now these
  statuses can only be changed directly in the DB — there's no UI to update them
